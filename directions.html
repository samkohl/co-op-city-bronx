Directions

Co-op City is near Pelham Bay Park, City Island and Orchard Beach.



Public Transportation


Co-op City is approximately 1 hour from Midtown Manhattan by Express Bus, local bus lines that stop at subway stations and subway.To get to Co-op City, take the BxM7 Express Bus from Manhattan or the No. 6 subway to the last stop and then transfer to either the Bx 23, Bx 12 Select or QBx 50 bus for a short trip to Co-op City.

By Car


There are exits from I-95, the Bruckner Expressway and the Hutchinson River Parkway to Co-op City.
