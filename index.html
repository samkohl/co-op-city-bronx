Co-Op City

The largest co-operative housing development in the world

History

Co-op City is a New York City Mitchell-Lama cooperative located in the Northeast Bronx. With 15,372 apartments in 35 high-rise buildings and 7 townhouse clusters, Co-op City has approximately 50,000 residents.


Accomodation

Co-op City has spacious one, two and three bedroom air conditioned apartments, many of which have beautiful views. Located on 320 acres next to Pelham Bay Park, the grounds are full of gardens, park benches, trees, playgrounds and ball fields.


Directions

Co-op City is near Pelham Bay Park, City Island and Orchard Beach. To get to Co-op City, take the BxM7 Express Bus from Manhattan or the No. 6 subway to the last stop and then transfer to either the Bx 23, Bx 12 Select or QBx 50 bus for a short trip to Co-op City.
